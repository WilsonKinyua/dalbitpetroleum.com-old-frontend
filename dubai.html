Dalbit Dubai

DIL Trading is a subsidiary of Dalbit International Limited, an entity incorporated in Mauritius and part of the Janus Continental Group (JCG).

Dalbit International Limited primarily engages in trading and distributing petroleum products in African markets and has active affiliations in multiple countries in East, Central and Southern Africa. DIL Trading, therefore, aims to continue expanding the Petroleum trading portfolio for Janus Continental Group by actively delivering end to end trading and logistics support for all clean petroleum products, including liquified natural gas, in the long run. We focus on all aspects of physical trading of gasoline, condensate, naphtha, gas oil, fuel oils and jet fuel across all grades, sizes, and geographies.

Operations

DIL Trading is strategically positioned to boost the Group's proximity to local refiners in the Arab Gulf, key traders from whom we buy the bulk of our trading products, and major shipping companies that support maritime activities for our key markets.

We differentiate ourselves by committing to long-term business relations and the application of innovative and reliable trading solutions. By combining our regional market knowledge with diverse logistics expertise, we have, over the years, earned a reputation for reliably delivering our customers' expectations. The company brings onboard trading executives with over a decade of experience in its target markets. It continues broadening its structure to build a solid team fit for the sustenance of its target growth in trading.
