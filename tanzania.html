Dalbit Tanzania

Dalbit Tanzania began operations in 2007. Today, we are one of the country’s biggest players, importing products through the port of Dar es Salaam.

We have hospitality agreements with key depots guaranteeing adequate stock ready for loading at all times to meet the customers’ needs. This ensures we can offer consistency of supply to our customers. Distribution is done through our managed fleet and reliable sub contracted transport companies across the Tanzanian market and into the neighboring Eastern Democratic Republic of Congo and Zambia.

We service clients in the mining sector, as well as retail stations, transporters, and other commercial users.

Operations

With a well organised supply chain in country, Dalbit Tanzania imports products through the port of Dar es Salaam. We have large storage facilities in various key depots, guaranteeing adequate stock is always ready for loading to meet customer needs. Distribution is carried out through our managed fleet and reliable sub-contracted transport companies across the Tanzanian market and into the neighbouring eastern Democratic Republic of Congo and Zambia.

Transport

We take great care in our product delivery to ensure it is both highly secure and timely. We work with reliable transport companies, closely monitored to ensure they keep to precise delivery schedules. The trucks are equipped and modified to cover challenging terrain so they can reach wherever you need your fuel, whenever you need it.

Infrastructure & Storage

As a total energy solutions provider, we assist clients in designing and building the best storage facilities for fuel efficiency and safety. We also train our clients in their operations, thereby giving them a strategic edge.
